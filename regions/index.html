What we're asking

Come and Try is the biggest membership push CAQ has run. We are widening it region by region once we know the system is coping, so the job now is to get every region organised and ready to go the moment we open it up.

Find your region below and open its page. You'll see your clubs, mostly pre-filled from what we had in April. Confirm or correct each one's Come and Try times, club days, host and fee, tick any club that isn't taking part, and hit send. It takes a few minutes and comes straight back to us.

No login, no fuss. Anything that needs a second look, we'll catch and sort out with you.

What this is for

Come and Try is bringing new people to clubs across Queensland. We are starting carefully and will widen the campaign across the regions once we know the system is coping — so the job now is to be organised and ready to go the moment we open your region up.

For each of your clubs, we need:

  • A club host — the person we coordinate with, and how to reach them.
  • Come and Try times — up to two set times a week when a newcomer can turn up for a taster.
  • Club play days — up to three regular playing days, so a newcomer knows when to come back.
  • Membership fee — roughly, so guests can be told what joining costs.

Most are pre-filled from what we had in April. Just confirm or correct them and hit submit at the bottom. If a club isn't taking part, tick Not participating. It all comes straight back to us.
